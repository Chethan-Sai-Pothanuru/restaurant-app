Our Story

A Legacy of Flavour & Craft

La Maison was born from a singular belief: that a great meal is a form of art. Our head chef, Élise Moreau, trained in Lyon and Tokyo before bringing her vision to life in the heart of the city.

Every dish is a meditation on ingredients — sourced from trusted local farms, prepared with classical technique, and plated with modern sensibility.
